The developers of konrad

We are PhD students, which means we get paid to study and do research, and one day we will be able to call ourselves doctors, even though we won't be qualified to help sick people.

We use konrad to study the climate and how it depends on different things. Lukas is interested in how the equilibrium surface temperature changes, when the amount of CO2 in the atmosphere increases, and how that change depends on different assumptions about water vapour and lapse rate changes, as well as changes in clouds. Sally studies the tropical tropopause layer and how it is influenced by ozone, tropical upwelling, convection and clouds.

We are building on the work of many other people and are following their ideas. Konrad uses lots of open source software developed by other people, and we wouldn't be able to do our research without this.
